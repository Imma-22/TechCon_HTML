History of TechCon

TechCon began in the early 2000 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has since grown into a globally recognized event attended by leaders and innovators in technology.

This image shows earlier days of TechCon, where tech professionals gathered to share knowledge and insights.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire the next generation by striving together to build a brighter future where innovation thrives, investments abound, entrepreneurship flourishes, and positive change permeates every aspect of society. Also, providing mentorship, resources, and networking opportunities to help them realize their aspirations.

TechCon embodies a commitment to fostering innovation, catalyzing investment, nurturing entrepreneurship, and influencing positive change.

Notable Past Speakers

Brain Mesic

Brain Mesic is the managing director of Ankona Capital and a renowned tech entrepreneur and innovator, known for his investments in technology companies including Android, TrueCar, RFMagic, and Entropic Communications.

Joe Kiani

Joe Kiani is the CEO of MASIMO, a global medical technology company,founded in 1989. He also serves as Chairman and CEO. His desire to develop medical technology that improves patient care and decreases medical errors led him to co-invent what is known as modern pulse oximetry.
